Our Story

From a Small Team
to a Regional Force

VisionSync IT was founded with a simple belief: Jordanian businesses deserve access to world-class technology solutions without having to look abroad.

Starting as a two-person freelance operation in 2020, we've grown into a full-service IT company with a dedicated team of developers, designers, and consultants. Our work spans e-commerce platforms, healthcare systems, fintech applications, and enterprise ERPs.

Today, we serve clients across Jordan, Saudi Arabia, UAE, and beyond — but our roots and our heart remain in Amman.
